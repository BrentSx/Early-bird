Our Story

Hamilton's Home for Breakfast & Brunch

Local & Regional Vendors

Nestled in Hamilton's historic German Village, Early Berds brings American breakfast classics to life in a warm European café atmosphere. Owner Logan Fitzwater — a Hamilton native — built this spot from the ground up with his grandfather, brick by brick, to give this city the seven-day breakfast destination it deserves.

From farm-fresh eggs to hand-crafted espresso sourced from Deeper Roots Coffee in Cincinnati, everything we serve is made with care and community in mind.
